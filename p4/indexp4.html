Meet Our Maker

My name is Andrew Davison and I’m owner, designer, and craftsman at Ptarmigan Custom woodwork and interiors. Being a self-taught woodworker since 1973 has allowed me to explore the craft and fine tune my skills.
You know what it means to invest in fine craftsmanship; to have something created with your needs in mind that will last a life time and still look and feel as good as the day you bought it. Let quality be your guide. Let Ptarmigan Custom woodwork and interiors get you there.

Our Commitment

Sustainability

In an age of increased environmental consciousness, bespoke wood furniture aligns with sustainable practices. By choosing bespoke wood furniture, customers can enjoy the beauty of nature while also contributing to a more sustainable future.

Hand-Crafted

Ptarmigan celebrates the art of traditional craftsmanship, embracing the skills and techniques handed down through generations. Each grain, knot, and curve is carefully considered, resulting in furniture that becomes a unique reflection of the client’s taste and style.

Timeless

Wood has a natural beauty that transcends passing trends, and bespoke wood furniture amplifies this timeless aesthetic. We harness these inherent qualities, transforming raw timber into works of art that become focal points within a space, effortlessly blending with any interior style.
